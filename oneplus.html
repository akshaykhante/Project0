Oneplus 8 pro


OnePlus 8 Pro smartphone was launched on 14th April 2020. The phone comes with a 6.78-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1440x3168 pixels at a pixel density of 513 pixels per inch (ppi) and an aspect ratio of 19.8:9. OnePlus 8 Pro is powered by an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 865 processor. It comes with 8GB of RAM. The OnePlus 8 Pro runs Android 10 and is powered by a 4510mAh non-removable battery. The OnePlus 8 Pro supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging.
