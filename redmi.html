Xiomi mi 10



Xiaomi recently launched the Mi 10 5G smartphone under its flagship Mi 10 series in China. The device comes in three RAM and storage variants: 8GB+128GB, 8GB+256GB and 12GB+256GB. There are three colour variants of the device: Titanium Silver Black, Peach Gold and Ice Blue. The base variant comes at 3,999 Chinese Yuan (approximately Rs 40,900), the 8GB+256GB variant, at 4,299 Chinese yuan (about Rs 43,968), while the 12GB+256GB variant has been priced at 4,699 Chinese yuan (about Rs 48,084)
